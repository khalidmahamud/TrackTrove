1) Show the track names with duration less than that of the track, 'Oniket Prantor'
Query:

SELECT t name, duration
 FROM tracks
  WHERE duration <
                 (SELECT duration
                   FROM tracks
                    WHERE t name = 'Oniket Prantor');

2) Show the album names and creation date that was created before t_id 345
Query:

SELECT album name, album date
 FROM has
  WHERE album date <
                   (SELECT t _ date
                     FROM tracks
                      WHERE t _ id = 345);
